Brick Breakout Game - Instructions to Play

The game consists of three levels:

  • Level 1(Beginner level)
  • Level 2(Intermediate level)
  • Level 3(Difficult level)
Level 1
Beginner/Easy level consists of single ball and single paddle and the number of lives provided for this level are three. Speed of ball is minimum for this level.
Level 2
Intermediate level consists of single ball and single paddle and the number of lives provided for this level are three. Speed of the ball is slightly greater than level one.
Level 3
Difficult level consists of two balls and single paddle and the number of lives provided for this level are ten. Speed of the ball is greater than level two.
