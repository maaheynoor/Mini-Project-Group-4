Brick Breaker (platformer) is a Breakout clone which the player must smash a wall of bricks by deflecting a bouncing ball with a paddle.
The paddle may move horizontally and is controlled with the BlackBerry's trackwheel, the computer's mouse or the touch of a finger (in the case of touchscreen).
The player gets 3 lives to start with; a life is lost if the ball hits the bottom of the screen.
When all the bricks have been destroyed, the player advances to a new, harder level. There are 3 levels. If all lives are lost, the game is over.
There are many versions of brick breaker, some in which you can shoot flaming fireballs or play with more than one ball if the player gets a power up.
Three levels of game:
Easy level:
Three layers of static bricks with single ball. Player wins the level if all the bricks are cleared. Speed of the ball is slow.
Medium level:
Bricks will gradually come down and the paddle size is relatively smaller than easy level. Player loses the game as the brick touches the bottom of the screen.
Player wins the level if all the bricks are cleared before they touch the paddle.
Hard level:
Bricks will gradually come down and the paddle size is relatively smaller than medium level. Number of balls is two and the speed of the ball is increased.
Three lives for each level
